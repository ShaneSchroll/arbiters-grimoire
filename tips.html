The Oracle: Tips

For more detailed rulings give the bot a board state along with the question.

Providing the board state improves reasoning as triggers and state interactions are very specific and will be explained along with the ruling.

Example:
"My board state: I have 5 Forests in play, Scute Swarm in play, and Roiling Regrowth on the stack. Do I get 2 copies of Scute Swarm since Roiling Regrowth resolves before the Scute Swarm trigger or do I get 1 insect and 1 copy based on lands in play when I played Roiling Regrowth, triggering Scute Swarm as each land comes into play?"

The Oracle will then break-down the order in which items trigger and resolve and how the board state progresses while explaining each one.

Capitalize Card Names

Capitalizing card names helps the bot determine named cards to perform an oracle text lookup for accurate rulings.
